About Us

CEC Bijni

CEC Bijni is one of the most popular and fastest-growing computer education centres in the Bijni area of Assam. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and smart digital classrooms, we are committed to delivering high-quality computer education that prepares students for the demands of the modern digital world. Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to advance your existing skills, CEC Bijni has the right course for you.

Our centre is led by a team of well-experienced faculty members who bring both academic knowledge and practical expertise to the classroom. With a strong reputation built on student success and community trust, CEC Bijni continues to grow as the preferred destination for computer learning in the region. We take pride in our modern infrastructure, supportive learning environment, and our unwavering dedication to student excellence.
